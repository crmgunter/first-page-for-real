About Me

I am passionate about helping others grow in their leadership, helping teams perform at higher levels, and helping organizations increase results. I serve individuals and organizations through coaching, leadership training, and team development facilitation. I’m a graduate of Reinhardt University and Berry College. I earned an Associate of Fine Arts degree from Reinhardt University, a Bachelor of Arts degree from Berry College, and a Master of Business Administration degree from the Campbell School of Business. I went on to start and run a successful team development company before going to work for Cobb-Vantress, Inc., as Director of Training and Leadership Development. Prior to this work, I was the Program Manager for WinShape Teams, a part of Chick-fil-A’s WinShape Foundation, specializing in building teams. I now work with individual leaders and organizations utilizing my skills and experience in business as a coach, trainer, and facilitator. I’m an adventurer at heart and enjoy summiting mountains, mountain biking, and exploring the outdoors. I love spending time with my awesome kids, creating and experiencing art, and living out my faith. You can contact me via e-mail or follow me on Twitter or Facebook.
